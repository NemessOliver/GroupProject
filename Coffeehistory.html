Our Coffee

From Cherry to Bean: The Milling Process

Once harvested, the coffee cherries go through a meticulous milling process to extract the prized beans inside. At OUI, we emphasize traditional methods. The cherries are washed and then dried naturally in the sun—a process called the washed method, known for enhancing the clarity and brightness of the coffee’s flavor. After drying, the beans are hulled to remove their parchment layer, revealing the raw green coffee beans underneath. These beans are then carefully sorted and graded based on size, density, and quality, ensuring only the finest make it into your cup.

The Art of Roasting

The next crucial step in the journey is roasting. This is where the magic truly happens. We roast our beans in small batches here in France to capture the unique characteristics of each origin. Our roast profiles are designed to bring out the bright acidity of Ethiopian beans, the caramel-like sweetness of Guatemalan coffee, and the smooth, nutty undertones of Colombian beans. Each batch is roasted to perfection, giving you a rich, aromatic experience that’s truly one of a kind.

From Our Baristas to Your Cup

Finally, our skilled baristas at OUI transform these roasted beans into the perfect cup of coffee. Whether it’s a velvety espresso, a creamy latte, or a classic café au lait, every sip is a celebration of the journey from the farm to your cup. We take pride in serving coffee that not only delights your senses but also supports sustainable farming and empowers coffee-growing communities around the world. At OUI, we invite you to experience more than just coffee. We invite you to taste tradition, passion, and quality in every sip.
